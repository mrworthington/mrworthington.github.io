Jesus Loves Monsters

 Posted on May 1, 2013
“Everybody wants to know what my achilles heel is. Love. I don’t get enough of it.” -Jay-Z in Monster- Frequently, I am convicted by Christ’s radical call to love our enemies. In his book The Importance of Being Foolish, Brennan Manning captures the extremity of his call for love: “The incontrovertible sign of Christians who have experienced forgiveness is the ability to love their enemies.” Generally, when we think of loving or forgiving our enemies, it tends to be in a somewhat temporal context.
